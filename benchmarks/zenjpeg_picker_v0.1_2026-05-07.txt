Notes:
- effort=1 is always the oracle pick (knob has near-zero byte-saving value
  on this corpus); 100% accuracy is the trivial constant predictor.
- chroma_distance_scale (only 0.7 vs 1.2 in this grid) is the noisiest
  axis (72% acc) — the picker correctly notices content-dependent
  preferences but doesn't yet capture the full signal. Worth densifying
  the chroma_distance_scale grid in the next sweep to see if a broader
  range (e.g. 0.5/0.8/1.0/1.4) gives a sharper signal.
- illustration_or_logo class underfits. 46% capture vs 78% for screens.
  The class is mostly logos/icons (gif-static converts) with sharp edges
  + flat colors — a regime where butteraugli/zensim disagree on what's
  "good" and the byte-savings frontier is steep but narrow. Likely needs
  more lineart-heavy training data or a loss reweighting.
